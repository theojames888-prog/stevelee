Plain-language summary: CutPro is a local video editor. We do not sell your data, we do not track you across other apps, and your video files never leave your device unless you explicitly export them.
01

Information We Collect

CutPro Video Editor (Bundle ID: com.appchunks.VideoEditor) is designed with your privacy as a default. We collect only the minimum information necessary to operate the app.

Information you provide:

  • Media files (video, audio, images) that you import — processed entirely on-device
  • Project files and editing data saved locally to your device
  • Export preferences and app settings

Information collected automatically:

  • Crash reports and diagnostic data (via Apple's opt-in system, if you have enabled "Share with App Developers" in your device settings)
  • Aggregate, anonymous usage statistics (e.g., which features are used most — no personally identifiable information)

We do not collect: your name, email, location, contacts, browsing history, or any media content from your library without explicit action.

02

How We Use Information

Any information collected is used solely to:

  • Provide and improve the core video editing functionality
  • Diagnose and fix crashes or technical issues
  • Understand which features are valuable to users (in aggregate, anonymously)
  • Respond to support requests you initiate

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating and improving the app.

03

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share limited data only in these circumstances:

  • Apple: Crash reports may be shared with Apple per their standard developer diagnostics program
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Business transfers: In the unlikely event of a merger or acquisition, data practices will be disclosed in advance
04

Data Storage & Security

All your video projects and media are stored locally on your device. We do not operate cloud servers that store your content.

If you use iCloud backup (controlled by your macOS/iOS settings), your project files may be included in your personal iCloud backup — this is governed by Apple's iCloud privacy policy, not ours.

We implement industry-standard security practices within the app and follow Apple's App Sandbox guidelines to prevent unauthorised access to your data.

05

Children's Privacy

CutPro is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

06

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability

Since most data is stored locally on your device, you can delete the app at any time to remove all locally stored data. For any other requests, contact us at the address below.
